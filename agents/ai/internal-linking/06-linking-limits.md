# Linking Limits

## Goal
Internal linking must stay useful, subtle, and strategic.

## Default limits per blog article
- max 4 inline text links
- max 1 intro / hero related link
- max 1 contextual card inside article body
- max 1 brand card
- max 1 conversion card
- max 3 related articles below the article
- max 1 sticky sidebar recommendation block

## Priority order
1. core cluster article or hub page
2. Hybrid Analysis page
3. supporting article
4. brand story or EXOPEK page
5. optional coach page

## Density rules
- no more than 1 internal link per paragraph
- no repeated target in nearby paragraphs
- no more than 2 links in one section unless explicitly allowed
- conversion links must remain subtle

## Article-type overrides
### knowledge article
- focus on supporting articles + hub
- only soft conversion

### method article
- stronger link to Train Hybrid system
- may include Hybrid Analysis CTA

### brand article
- stronger brand story / EXOPEK linking allowed

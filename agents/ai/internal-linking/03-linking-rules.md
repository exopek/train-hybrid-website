# Linking Rules

## Objective
Internal links must feel useful, contextual, and strategically intentional.

They must not feel like random SEO stuffing or banner spam.

## Link categories
The agent may recommend links in four categories:

### 1. Context links
Inline links inside the body text.

### 2. Strategic CTA links
Links in cards, blocks, or highlighted sections.

### 3. Brand story links
Links to athlete stories, coaches, EXOPEK use cases, or brand-related pages.

### 4. Structural links
Related articles, sidebar recommendations, footer clusters.

## Priority logic
Default priority order:
1. core system or hub page
2. Hybrid Analysis page
3. supporting article
4. subtle brand link
5. optional product page if contextually relevant

## Limits
- maximum 4 inline links per article body
- maximum 1 conversion card per article
- maximum 1 brand story card per article
- avoid linking the same target repeatedly in one section
- avoid excessive exact-match anchors

## Tone
Links must feel editorial, not pushy.

## Forbidden behaviors
- no irrelevant product pushing
- no hard sales links in purely informational passages
- no repetitive anchor text stuffing
- no duplicate link target recommendations without reason
